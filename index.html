Research

My research interests lie at the intersection of digital technology and human behavior. Currenty, I am working on these following projects.

Heterophily on Social Networking Sites. I examine the tendency to develop diverse networks or known as heterophily, concerning race and political ideology on social networking sites such as Facebook and Twitter. I aim to answer, “What models explain and predict individual’s tendency to develop diverse networks beyond a mere chance?”

Racial Identity on Social Media. I study how people identify themselves as members of particular race or ethnicity on Twitter. Even though those construcst are salient in face-to-face interactions, it is not the case with online world. Hence, I am interested to investigate motivations and reasons associated with such identity. Why do some people deliberately put racial identity (e.g., black, Asian) on their Twitter bios while others hide it?
